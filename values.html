Values & Impact

Rooted in Tradition, Serving the Community

Our Doctrine

Our values and teachings are strictly in line with the doctrine set by Imam Ahmed Rida Khan and the pious predecessors (Salaf al-Salihin). We believe in a balanced, traditional approach to Islam that emphasizes love for the Prophet (saw) and adherence to the Shari'ah.

Regional Impact

By the grace of Allah Almighty, JGR College graduates are now leading young scholars and Imams in Masjids throughout the North West. Our impact is seen in the pulpits and classrooms across the region, where our students continue the work of serving the deen.
